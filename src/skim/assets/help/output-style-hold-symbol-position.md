# Hold Symbol Position

Controls where the hold function indicator appears on dual-function keys (keys
that have both a tap and hold action).

- **Outward** — hold indicator faces away from the center of the keyboard
- **Inward** — hold indicator faces toward the center
- **QMK** — follows the argument order of the QMK macro functions that define
  the keycode (e.g. `LT(1, KC_A)` will render the hold on the left side
  and the tap on the right).
